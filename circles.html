Composeo

Composeo started as a partnership between ENSEIRB-MATMECA (my engineering school at the time) and Sopra-Group. It aimed at creating a clone of Netvibes or iGoogle (in a nutshell, personal page with tabs and widgets) that companies could install as their intranet interface. It would allow them to code widgets with confidential data without having to send these data to an untrusted third-party.

We were a team of 6 during one semester and 12 during another semester.

The project was discontinued and sources are not available, but it was my first by web project and I learned a lot in reproducing more or less Netvibes.
